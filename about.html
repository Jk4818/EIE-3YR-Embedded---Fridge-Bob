About Us

We are a student led start-up on a mission to combat the biggest crisis of our lifetimes: climate change.

We developed Fridge-Bob to combat food wastage and help households move towards sustainability by throwing away less food.

Our mission is to grow Fridge Bob into a device in every fridge that can help reduce food wastage by keeping your fridge in optimal condition.
We also aim to keep you, our customers, happy with a product that helps you save money by throwing away less food and
making you aware and proud of the difference you and Fridge Bob are making in the world!
